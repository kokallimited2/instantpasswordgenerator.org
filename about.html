Mission

Instant Password Generator exists to provide IT administrators and DevOps teams with a free, cryptographically secure bulk password generation tool with zero server-side processing. Every password is generated by your browser using crypto.getRandomValues() — the same entropy source as enterprise HSMs. Our writing covers the operational side: PowerShell automation, CSV pipelines, Active Directory provisioning, and NIST policy implementation.
