1. Who We Are

InstantPasswordGenerator.org is operated by Kokal Operations Ltd, registered in England and Wales. We are the data controller for personal information collected through this website. Contact: hello@instantpasswordgenerator.org

2. What Data We Collect

What we do NOT collect

  • Passwords — all generation uses crypto.getRandomValues() client-side; no passwords reach our servers.
  • Keystroke or typing data of any kind.

Data collected automatically

Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) logs standard access data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps. We do not use this to identify individuals.

Data you provide voluntarily

  • Contact form: name, email, subject, message
  • Cookie preference stored in localStorage as ipg-ck — never transmitted

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies. Your consent choice is stored in browser localStorage under ipg-ck — never transmitted to any server. See our Cookie Policy.

6. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your data. Email privacy@instantpasswordgenerator.org to exercise any right. We respond within 30 days. You may also contact the ICO at ico.org.uk.
